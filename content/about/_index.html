--- title: "About" date: 2018-05-04T20:16:52+02:00 draft: true --- Ed Warnicke is a Distinguished Consulting Engineer in the Chief Technology and Architecture Office (CTAO) office at Cisco Systems. He has been working for over a decade in many areas of networking and Open Source. He has been a member of the OpenDaylight TSC since its inception and currently serves as a committer elected member of the OpenDaylight TSC. He is a founding TSC member at ONAP. Ed has a masters in Physics (String Theory) from Rutgers University.
